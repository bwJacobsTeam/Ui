SAVE THE ANIMALS

There are thousands of conservation organizations all over the world working tirelessly to stop extinction and they need our help.

Who We Are

We are wildlife biologists who saw a massive disconnect in the way we could reach out for real-time global support for critical needs outside of our project's budget. We also knew conservation organizations all over the world were facing similar issues.

Additionally, supporters of conservation organizations want more transparency as to where their money is going, what the outcome of their support is, as well as better ways to stay connected.

How Can We Help?

Key Conservation is helping conservationists gain critical funding and global support through a mobile app we are currently developing. Organizations can create campaigns to request aid and then be linked to support groups for financial or local in-person assistance.

How Did This Idea Come About?

It all started when we were working on a sea turtle project together in the Caribbean. The project had a hotline where locals and tourists could call in any problems or sightings they had of sea turtles. One day we received a call about a nest that was being washed out to sea. We ran out to our patrol vehicle to head down to the nest only to realize we were about to be out of gas.

The problem was our project did not have any money left; We would have to wait until the following week for funds to come through. We could not just let the nest wash out to sea so we used the small stipend we had for our own food to fill the tank. On the way out we grabbed some project shirts, hoping we could sell them to cover our costs.

During the drive to the nest, all we could think about was how there had to be a better way to reach out to the world to get real-time support. We knew at least 5 people who would have given us $20 to fill our gas tank, just so they could tell their friends they helped save baby sea turtles in the Caribbean that day.
